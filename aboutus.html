About Us

The Couple's Farmyard is a ranch located at Sulvec, Narvacan, Ilocos Sur, Philippines; a farm tour destination adorned with outdoor facilities, cycling track, mini golf and assembly hall. You can also get a chance to pick seasonal vegetables and fruits, or served by our vegan resto. Whether you want an exclusive and peaceful nature experience or you want to engage in fun group activities and adventures, indulge among Ilocos Sur's landscapes by booking an AirBnB among our lodging accommodations or camp out to spend a quiet night or two under the stars.
